The Lotus Foundation
See the Project

How we created a unique design, and developed the website for a non profit organization.

Logo & Palette

The organization's current logo was already provided and we created a color palette based on the colors present in the logo.


More on the usage of the color palette later.

 
 

Wireframing

The first step was to start with a few wireframes to capture the most important sections of the website as per the requirements.


Designing without color is great at this stage since everyone gets to concentrate on the layouts and structures.

Design Mockup

The second step in design was to create a mockup based on the approved wireframes.


Stock images were used, and we finally made use of the color palette.
