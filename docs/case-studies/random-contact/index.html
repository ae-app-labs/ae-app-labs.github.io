THE CHALLENGE

Redesign the app to improve user experience and add additional features.

Random Contact App

This is a hobby Android project that enables its users to see a random contact from their contacts list.


It started out as an experiment and now has been downloaded more than 1000 times.

A fresh coat of paint

Random Contact version 3 was designed based on the Google Material Design guidelines back when it launched for the first time.


Since then the design guidelines have evolved and better theme support, as well as dark mode became available.

 

Design Approach

Armed with the powerful Adobe XD to design and wireframe, we started out with a basic wireframe of all the flows required for the app.


As always starting with the wireframe is the best approach to capture all the essentials for the UX.


There were some shortcomings of the previous design such as Custom Group Management hidden underneath a hanmburger menu.

 

Mocking Up

Moving away from the colorful and Card style design to a more modern and streamlined design was always the plan.


The colors of the icon are used carefully as accents in the interface.


The toolbar was cleaned up in the final design and made use of a bottom navigation bar.

 

Dark Mode

The latest Android design guidelines and resources makes it quite easy to provide users with light and dark themes. The light theme mockup was used as a basis to design a dark variant theme.
